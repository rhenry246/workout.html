12-Week Progressive Overload Program

Training Schedule

Select a Training Phase

Focus (Weeks 1-4): Higher volume and moderate intensity to build work capacity and stimulate sarcoplasmic hypertrophy.

Focus (Weeks 5-8): Increasing load and mechanical tension to drive myofibrillar hypertrophy and strength.

Focus (Weeks 9-12): Maximizing strength to establish a new, higher baseline for the next cycle.

Week 13: Deload

Focus on active recovery to dissipate fatigue and prepare for the next cycle. Follow the plan for Week 12 (Phase 3), but with these critical modifications:

  • Reduce the number of sets for each exercise by half (e.g., 5 sets becomes 2 or 3).
  • Keep the weight the same as your last session.
  • Focus on perfect, crisp, and controlled repetitions. Do not train to failure.
  • After this week, restart the cycle at Phase 1, aiming to use heavier weights than in the previous cycle.

This program's success hinges on one principle. Use the smallest increment possible (e.g., 5 lbs / 2.5 kg). Track your lifts to ensure you are consistently getting stronger.
